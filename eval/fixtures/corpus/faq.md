# Northwind Coffee Supply — Frequently Asked Questions

## What does Northwind Coffee Supply sell?

Northwind Coffee Supply sells home and small-office espresso machines,
burr grinders, and freshly roasted coffee beans. Our flagship products are
the Northwind Brewline home espresso machine and the Northwind GrindPro
conical burr grinder. We also stock cleaning supplies, descaling solution,
water filters, and replacement parts.

## Where do you ship from?

All orders ship from our warehouse in Portland, Oregon. Coffee beans are
roasted to order in the same facility and dispatched within 24 hours of
roasting so they arrive fresh.

## Do you offer a subscription for coffee beans?

Yes. The Northwind Coffee Club delivers freshly roasted beans on a schedule
you choose — every one, two, or four weeks. Subscribers save 15% on every
bag and can pause or skip a delivery at any time from the account dashboard.

## Can I buy replacement parts separately?

Most wearable parts — portafilter baskets, group head gaskets, water tank
lids, and grinder burrs — can be purchased individually from the Parts &
Accessories section of the store. If a part is out of stock, contact support
and we will give you an estimated restock date.

## How do I contact support?

You can reach the Northwind support team by email at help@northwindcoffee.example
or through the live chat widget on our website. Support is available Monday to
Friday, 8am to 6pm Pacific Time. We aim to answer every message within one
business day.

## Is my machine covered if I use third-party beans?

Yes. Using third-party coffee beans does not void your warranty. However,
using non-Northwind descaling products that contain harsh acids can damage the
boiler and is not covered. We recommend only food-safe descaling solution.
