# Account & Billing

## Creating an account

You can create a Northwind account at checkout or from the Sign Up link in the
top navigation. An account lets you track orders, manage your Coffee Club
subscription, save shipping addresses, and start returns without contacting
support.

## Accepted payment methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit and debit
cards, as well as PayPal and Apple Pay. Coffee Club subscriptions must be paid
with a credit card or PayPal so that recurring charges can be processed
automatically.

## Managing your Coffee Club subscription

You can change your delivery frequency, swap the coffee roast, update your
payment method, skip the next delivery, or cancel your subscription at any time
from the Subscriptions tab in your account dashboard. Changes made before the
cutoff — 48 hours before your next scheduled roast — apply to the upcoming
delivery.

## When you are charged

For one-time orders, your card is charged when the order is placed. For Coffee
Club subscriptions, your card is charged each cycle on the day your beans are
roasted, not on the day you originally signed up. You will receive an email
receipt for every charge.

## Updating billing information

To update the card on file, go to Account, then Payment Methods, and add or
edit a card. If a subscription payment fails, we retry the charge for three
days and email you a link to update your card before pausing the subscription.

## Requesting an invoice

Business customers can download a detailed invoice for any order from Order
History. If you need a custom invoice with your company details or a tax ID,
contact support and we will prepare one for you.
