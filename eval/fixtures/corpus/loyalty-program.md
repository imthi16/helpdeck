# Northwind Beans & Points — Loyalty Program

## Earning points

Every registered customer earns 1 point per dollar spent on coffee,
accessories, and cleaning supplies, and 2 points per dollar on machines and
grinders. Points appear in your account within 24 hours of an order shipping.
Subscription deliveries earn points too. Points are not earned on gift
cards, shipping fees, or taxes.

## Redeeming points

Points are redeemed at checkout: 100 points equals $5 off. Redemptions come
in fixed steps (100, 250, and 500 points); you cannot redeem partial steps.
Points cannot be exchanged for cash and cannot be applied to gift card
purchases or wholesale orders.

## Point expiry

Points expire 18 months after they are earned if your account has no
qualifying purchase in that period. Any purchase — of any size — resets the
expiry clock for your entire balance. We email a reminder 30 days before any
points are due to expire.

## Birthday reward

Add your birthday to your account profile and we will drop a 250-gram bag of
our seasonal roast into your next order placed within your birthday month,
free. One birthday bag per customer per year; a shipped order in the
birthday month is required.

## Referral bonus

Share your referral link from the account dashboard. When a friend places
their first order of $40 or more, they get $10 off and you receive 300
points. Referral points are capped at 3,000 points (ten successful
referrals) per calendar year.

## Combining with other discounts

Points redemptions stack with the Coffee Club subscriber discount but cannot
be combined with one-time promotional codes. If a promotional code offers a
larger discount, checkout applies the better of the two automatically.
