# Wholesale & Office Program

## Who qualifies

The Northwind wholesale program is open to cafés, restaurants, offices, and
retailers ordering at least 10 kg of roasted coffee per month or five or more
machines per year. Apply through the Wholesale page with your business
details and resale certificate where applicable; applications are reviewed
within three business days.

## Wholesale pricing

Wholesale coffee is priced in three tiers by monthly volume: 10–25 kg saves
20% off retail, 25–100 kg saves 30%, and over 100 kg is quoted individually
by your account manager. Machines and grinders for qualified accounts are
discounted 15% off retail. Wholesale prices are confidential under the
program agreement.

## Ordering and payment terms

Wholesale orders are placed through the wholesale portal, not the regular
store. Approved accounts in good standing can pay on Net 30 terms after
their first three prepaid orders; new accounts prepay by card or bank
transfer. Orders placed by 12:00 PM Pacific on a roasting day (Monday,
Wednesday, Friday) ship the same day.

## Equipment support for wholesale accounts

Wholesale accounts receive priority support with a four-business-hour
response target, and machines used commercially keep a 1-year commercial
warranty instead of the standard 2-year home warranty. On-site service is
available in the Portland metro area for a call-out fee.

## Office coffee program

The office program includes a Brewline machine on free loan for offices
subscribing to at least 8 kg of beans per month for twelve months. The loaner
machine is serviced by us annually at no cost; if the subscription ends
early, the machine can be purchased at a prorated price or returned.
