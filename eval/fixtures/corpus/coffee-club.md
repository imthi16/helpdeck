# Northwind Coffee Club — Subscription Guide

## How the subscription works

The Northwind Coffee Club delivers freshly roasted beans on a schedule you
choose: every one, two, or four weeks. Every subscription bag ships within 24
hours of roasting. Subscribers save 15% on every bag compared to one-off
prices, and subscription orders always ship free regardless of order value.

## Choosing and changing your coffee

You can subscribe to any single-origin or blend we roast. Swap the coffee in
your next delivery from the account dashboard any time before the "roasting
soon" cutoff, which is 48 hours before your scheduled roast date. After the
cutoff the next bag is locked in, but all later deliveries can still be
changed.

## Pausing, skipping, and cancelling

You can pause your subscription for up to three months, skip any single
delivery, or cancel entirely from the account dashboard — there is no minimum
commitment and no cancellation fee. Cancelling takes effect immediately for
any delivery that has not yet reached its 48-hour roasting cutoff.

## Coffee Club perks

After six consecutive deliveries, Coffee Club members are upgraded to Club
Gold. Club Gold members get free express shipping upgrades, early access to
limited micro-lot releases, and a free 250-gram anniversary bag once a year.

## Gifting a subscription

Gift subscriptions are available in 3, 6, and 12-delivery packages, paid up
front. The recipient can choose the coffee and schedule; gift subscriptions
do not auto-renew, so there is never a surprise charge at the end.

## Billing for subscriptions

Subscriptions are billed per delivery on the day the beans are roasted, not
when the order is placed. The card on file in your account dashboard is used;
if a charge fails we retry for three days and email you a link to update your
card before pausing the subscription.
