# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Where we ship

Northwind Coffee Supply ships to all fifty United States, Canada, and the
European Union. We do not currently ship to other destinations, but we are
working on expanding coverage in the future.

## Processing time

Orders for in-stock hardware and accessories are processed within one business
day. Coffee bean orders are roasted to order and dispatched within 24 hours of
roasting. Orders placed before 12:00 PM Pacific Time on a business day usually
ship the same day.

## Delivery estimates

Standard shipping within the United States takes three to five business days.
Expedited shipping takes one to two business days and is available at checkout
for an additional fee. Delivery to Canada takes five to eight business days,
and delivery to the European Union takes seven to ten business days, plus any
time held in customs.

## Free shipping threshold

Orders over 75 US dollars qualify for free standard shipping within the United
States. The free shipping threshold is calculated after any discounts and
coupons are applied, and before taxes. Free shipping does not apply to
expedited orders or international destinations.

## Tracking your order

As soon as your order ships you will receive an email with a tracking number
and a link to follow the package. You can also find tracking information under
Order History in your account dashboard. If tracking has not updated for more
than three business days, please contact support.

## Customs and import duties

For orders shipped to Canada or the European Union, the recipient is
responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees charged by the
destination country. These fees are not included in the order total and are
collected by the carrier or local authorities on delivery.
