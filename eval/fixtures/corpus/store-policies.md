# Store Policies — Orders, Changes & Price Promises

## Changing or cancelling an order

Orders can be changed or cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, from Order
History in the account dashboard. After the 1-hour window the warehouse may
have started picking and the order can no longer be modified — if that
happens, use the normal returns process once it arrives. Bean orders enter
the roasting queue at the 1-hour mark and cannot be cancelled afterwards.

## Wrong or damaged items

If an order arrives damaged or with the wrong item, contact support within 7
days of delivery with a photo of the item and the shipping box. We ship the
correct replacement immediately at no cost and, for damaged items, we do not
require the damaged unit back unless support asks for it.

## Out-of-stock and backorders

If an item goes out of stock after you order, we email you within one
business day with the choice of waiting for the restock, swapping to an
equivalent item, or an immediate full refund. Backordered machines are
prioritized in first-paid, first-shipped order.

## Price matching

We match the advertised price of any authorized Northwind retailer at the
time of purchase. Send support a link to the competitor listing; matches
exclude auction sites, refurbished units, and bundle pricing. Price matching
cannot be combined with promotional codes.

## Gift cards

Digital gift cards are delivered by email within minutes and never expire.
Gift cards can be used for any product except other gift cards and cannot be
redeemed for cash except where required by law. Lost physical gift cards can
be reissued if you have the original order number.

## Holiday cutoffs

Ground-shipping orders placed by December 15 arrive by December 24 in the
contiguous United States. Express cutoff is December 20. Roast-to-order
beans follow the same cutoffs; during the holiday peak we roast seven days a
week.
