# Payments, Pricing & Account Security

## Accepted payment methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google
Pay, and PayPal. Wholesale accounts can additionally pay by ACH bank
transfer. We do not accept cryptocurrency, checks, or cash on delivery.

## When you are charged

One-off orders are charged when the order is placed. Subscription deliveries
are charged on roast day, not at signup. Pre-orders for limited releases are
charged at checkout to reserve your lot.

## Price adjustments

If an item you bought at full price is discounted within 7 days of your
purchase, contact support with your order number and we will refund the
difference to your original payment method. Price adjustments do not apply
to flash sales shorter than 24 hours or to clearance items.

## Sales tax

We collect sales tax where legally required based on the shipping address.
Tax-exempt organizations can upload their exemption certificate on the
wholesale portal; exemption applies to orders placed after the certificate
is approved.

## Keeping your account secure

Enable two-factor authentication from Account Settings → Security. We will
never ask for your password by email or phone. Password resets always come
from northwindcoffee.example — treat any other sender as phishing and
forward it to security@northwindcoffee.example.

## Saved cards and data

Card details are stored by our PCI-DSS Level 1 payment processor, never on
Northwind servers — we keep only the last four digits and expiry for
display. You can remove a saved card at any time from the account dashboard,
and deleting your account removes stored payment profiles within 30 days.
