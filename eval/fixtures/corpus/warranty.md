# Warranty & Repairs

## Warranty coverage

Every Northwind espresso machine and grinder includes a two-year limited
manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. The
warranty starts on the date of purchase and covers parts and labor for
qualifying repairs.

## What the warranty does not cover

The warranty does not cover damage caused by accident, misuse, or neglect. It
does not cover normal wear on consumable parts such as gaskets, seals, and
grinder burrs. Damage from limescale buildup due to skipped descaling is not
covered, and neither is damage from using harsh third-party descaling
chemicals.

## How to make a warranty claim

To make a warranty claim, contact support with your order number and a
description of the problem. Our team may ask for photos or a short video to
diagnose the issue. If the machine is covered, we will arrange a repair or send
a replacement part, and we cover the cost of shipping both ways.

## Out-of-warranty repairs

If your machine is out of warranty, we still offer paid repair service. Contact
support for a repair quote. Many common issues, such as a worn group head
gasket or a clogged shower screen, can be fixed at home with an inexpensive
replacement part and our step-by-step guides.

## Extended warranty

You can purchase a one-year extended warranty within 60 days of your original
purchase. The extended warranty adds a third year of coverage on the same terms
as the standard warranty. It can be added from the product page or by contacting
support.
