# Returns & Refunds

## Return window

You may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The
30-day window starts on the date the carrier marks the package as delivered.
Items must be unused, in resalable condition, and returned in their original
packaging.

## Items that cannot be returned

Opened bags of coffee beans cannot be returned for hygiene reasons. Descaling
solution, water filters, and other consumables cannot be returned once the seal
is broken. Gift cards are non-refundable. Custom-engraved machines are final
sale and cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

## How to start a return

To start a return, open your account dashboard, go to Order History, select the
order, and choose Start a Return. We will email you a prepaid return shipping
label within one business day. Pack the item securely, attach the label, and
drop it off at any authorized carrier location.

## Refund timing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we issue the refund to your original
payment method within five business days. It can take an additional three to
five business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your
bank. We will email you when the refund has been processed.

## Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges. If you want a different item, please return
the original for a refund and place a new order. This ensures you get the new
item as quickly as possible without waiting for the return to arrive first.

## Damaged or defective items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact support within seven days of
delivery with a photo of the damage. We will send a replacement at no cost or
issue a full refund, including any original shipping charges.
